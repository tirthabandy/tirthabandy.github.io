Local Exploration

This work tries to look into the efficiency of a purely local exploration strategy. The robot looks at visibility edges and identifies the edges to move towards that uncovers the largest area due to its motion. This approach generated intersting pathways to explore the unknown but bounded region.
A distributed multi-robot strategy was also explored.
