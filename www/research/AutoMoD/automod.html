I am currently a Post. Doctoral Associate under Prof. Emilio Frazzoli and Prof. Daniel Rus at the Future Urban Mobility, (FM), at Singapore MIT Alliance for Research and Technology, S.M.A.R.T. I am currently investigating the utilization of autonomous vehicles in improving personal mobility in urban environments.

Prior to FM, I worked with Prof. Franz Hover and Prof. N. Patrikalakis in CENSAM on developing robust navigation techniques for marine autonomous surface vehicles for Harbor like environments. We operated in Singapore waters to deploy and test our autonomous kayak.

I did my Ph.D in Motion Planning for Target Tracking at the National University of Singapore under the supervision of Prof. David HSU and Prof. Marcelo ANG. My research dealt with developing motion strategies in 2-D as well as in 3-D, to track and follow a target of unknown intentions in an unknown environment, using only local information.
