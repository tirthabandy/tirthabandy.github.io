Autonomy for Mobility on Demand Systems

Demonstration Location

We ran autonomous mobility on demand service in NUS campus for very distinguished guests and visitors. The route requires the vehicle to perform navigation through pedestrian crossings, intersections, regions crowded by pedestrians. For the current demos, the vehicle uses the help from on-board driver for navigating intersections in traffic and we are developing decision strategies to make the system fully autonomous.
Since our move to the new campus at CREATE, we have setup fully autonomous operational trips between key locations in the Town Plaza. The video of the vehicle in operation among multiple moving pedestrians is shown here.
